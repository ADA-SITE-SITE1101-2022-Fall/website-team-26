Hour of Code Project

We, as Team 26 delievered a coding lesson to a group of school students as a part of Hour of Code movemment. The event was conducted on 15th November at 8 am at Baku Secondary School N0. 39.
We started the lesson with a presentation about ADA University. Next, we taught coding using activities on code.org. At the end we handed out certificates.
Our students did not have difficulty following the lesson and easily grasped programming concepts covered during the class. Upon finishing the lesson, they seemed interested in continuing learning with code.org.
